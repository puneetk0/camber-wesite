Installation

Download.
Drag. Race.

Ships as a universal binary. Runs natively on Apple Silicon and Intel. No account, no wizard, no fuss.

Download for macOS
1
Download from GitHub Releases
Grab the latest .dmg from the releases page — always free, always open source.
2
Open DMG and drag to Applications
Standard macOS install. Takes about five seconds.
3
Allow Camber in Privacy & Security (Imp)
macOS may block the app on first launch. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click "Open Anyway" next to Camber. You'll only need to do this once.
4
Hover the notch
Your pit lane is ready. Start your engines.
